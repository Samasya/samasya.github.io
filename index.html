What we do at Samasya

Samasya is a mathematics discussion and problem solving club. We discuss a variety of mathematical topics and solve problems as well. To initiate discussions, we post some problems in the middle of the week (usually around wednesday night) and we begin by trying to solve the problems. In the subsequent week, we discuss our solutions (if we managed to solve the problems) and our attempts at possible solutions.

When do we meet?

We usually meet every Friday, at 9:00 p.m., usually in the Old Physics Building's LAN room. The meetings last for 3 to 4 hours, and at around 11 p.m., we discuss the solutions of the previous week's problems. Of course, one is free to leave at any time wish, and some people might prefer to try the problems for more than a week, so they may prefer to skip the solution discussion.

The next meeting will be held on Friday, 2nd October, at 9:00 p.m. in the OPB LAN room.
